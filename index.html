About Me

Jiwei Hou received the M.Sc. degree from the School of Geomatics and Urban Spatial Information at Beijing University of Civil Engineering and Architecture, China, in 2019. He is currently pursuing his PhD degree in the Laboratory of Remote Sensing and Image Analysis at the Technical University of Darmstadt, Germany. His research interests include 3D seamless indoor and outdoor mapping and navigation.
